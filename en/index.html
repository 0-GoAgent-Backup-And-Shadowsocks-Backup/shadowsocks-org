A secure socks5 proxy,

designed to protect your Internet traffic.

  Try it now!  Get support

We believe an uncensored Internet is a better Internet.

  • Super Fast

    Bleeding edge techniques with Asynchronous I/O and Event-driven programming.

  • Flexible Encryption

    All traffic encrypted with industry level algorithms. Flexible to support custom encryption methods.

  • Mobile Ready

    Optimized for mobile device and wireless network, without keep-alive connections.

  • Cross Platform

    Avaliable on multiple platforms, including PC, MAC, Mobile (Android and iOS) and Routers (OpenWRT).

  • Open Source

    Open source implementations in python, node.js, golang, C#, and pure C.

  • Easy Deployment

    Easy deployment with pip, npm, aur,freshportsand many other package manager systems.
